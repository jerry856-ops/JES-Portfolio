About

A lens pointed
at everything

"I don't photograph categories — I photograph feelings."

I'm Jeremy. Sixteen years old, German by roots, Slovak by circumstance. I arrived in Lubeník as a missionary and discovered something I didn't expect — the time and space to really look at the world around me.

My work moves across landscapes, weddings, portraits, cars, fashion, and events. Not because I'm trying to cover every genre — but because I haven't found a reason to stop at just one. Photography and film are how I process the places I end up in, the people I meet, the light I find.

I'm 16. I'm still learning. And I think that's exactly why I still notice things that others have already stopped seeing.
